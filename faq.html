Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about preschool.

What is the ideal age to start preschool?
+

Children typically start between 1.5–3 years depending on readiness. Our Playgroup caters to 1.5+ with gentle settling and sensory play.

How can I prepare my toddler for preschool?
+

Start short separation practices, encourage simple routines (handwashing, snack time), read books daily, and practice simple self-help tasks.

What should I look for in a quality preschool?
+

Safe environment, trained teachers, play-based curriculum, low student-teacher ratio, hygiene & clear communication with parents.

What is the teacher-to-child ratio?
+

We maintain ratios: Playgroup 1:6, Nursery 1:8, Pre-Primary 1:10 to provide focused attention.

Do you provide meals and manage allergies?
+

Yes — nutritious meals and snacks provided. Please share allergies at enrollment; strict protocols prevent cross contamination.

How do I enroll my child?
+

Complete the enquiry form and our team will contact you for a tour, fee details, and admission steps.
