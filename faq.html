Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our preschool

What age groups do you accept?

We accept children from 1.5 years to 6 years across our five programs: Playgroup (1.5-3 years), Nursery (3-4 years), LKG (4-5 years), UKG (5-6 years), and Daycare (1.5+ years).

What are your operating hours?

Regular programs run from 9:00 AM to 1:00 PM, Monday to Friday. Our Daycare program extends until 7:00 PM for working parents. Saturday sessions are available from 9:00 AM to 1:00 PM.

What curriculum do you follow?

We follow the Early Years Foundation Stage (EYFS) curriculum, which is internationally recognized for its holistic approach to early childhood development. It focuses on learning through play while building academic, social, and emotional skills.

Do you provide meals?

Yes, nutritious snacks and meals are provided. Our Daycare program includes breakfast, lunch, and evening snacks. All meals are freshly prepared, balanced, and suitable for young children.

What is your teacher-to-student ratio?

We maintain a low teacher-to-student ratio of 1:10 to ensure individual attention for each child. For younger age groups (Playgroup), the ratio is even lower at 1:8.

Do you have CCTV monitoring?

Yes, our entire facility is equipped with CCTV cameras for security. Parents can request access to live feeds through our secure parent portal, ensuring complete transparency and peace of mind.

How do I enroll my child?

You can enroll by visiting our school, calling us at +91 9032249494, or filling out the contact form on our website. We recommend scheduling a school tour first to meet our teachers and see our facilities.
