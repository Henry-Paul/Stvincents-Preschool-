Our Curriculum & Programs

A foundation for lifelong learning through a play-based curriculum and caring teachers.

Playgroup

Target Age: 1.5 – 2.5 Years

Core Objective: Social & Sensory Exploration. Gentle introduction to school.

  • Separation comfort & secure attachments
  • Sensory play — sand, water, textures
  • Basic routines & independent play

Nursery

Target Age: 2.5 – 3.5 Years

Core Objective: Foundation literacy & numeracy through play.

  • Pre-reading & phonics awareness
  • Number recognition & counting
  • Confidence in group activities

Pre-Primary 1 (PP1)

Target Age: 3.5 – 4.5 Years

Core Objective: Pre-writing, phonemic awareness & concept application.

  • Letter formation & pre-writing strokes
  • Phonemic activities & group play
  • Early numeracy concepts

Pre-Primary 2 (PP2)

Target Age: 4.5 – 6 Years

Core Objective: Reading fluency, arithmetic basics & school readiness.

  • Reading fluency & comprehension
  • Basic addition & subtraction
  • Independent work & social readiness

Day Care

Target Age: 1.5 – 6 Years

Core Objective: Safe, structured extended care for working parents.

  • Meals & supervised rest
  • Structured activities & safety
  • Continuity of learning

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about preschool and early childhood development.

What is the ideal age to start preschool?
+

Children typically start between 1.5–3 years depending on readiness. Our Playgroup caters to 1.5+ with gentle settling and sensory play.

How can I prepare my toddler for preschool?
+

Start short separation practices, encourage simple routines (handwashing, snack time), read books daily, and practice simple self-help tasks.

What should I look for in a quality preschool?
+

Safe environment, trained teachers, play-based curriculum, low student-teacher ratio, hygiene & clear communication with parents.

How does preschool help with separation anxiety?
+

Gradual settling, consistent routines, familiar staff, and parent partnership help children feel secure and reduce separation anxiety.

What is the teacher-to-child ratio?
+

We maintain ratios designed to provide focused attention: Playgroup 1:6, Nursery 1:8, Pre-Primary 1:10 (approx.).

Do you provide meals and manage allergies?
+

Yes — nutritious meals and snack plans are provided. Please share allergy details at enrolment; strict protocols are followed to prevent cross contamination.

How do you handle emergencies or sickness?
+

First-aid trained staff, parent notification protocols, and quick access to local medical support. Serious cases are immediately escalated.

What are your school timings and fees structure?
+

Typical timings: Playgroup & Nursery 9 AM–12 PM, Pre-Primary 9 AM–1 PM. Daycare extension available. Please enquire for detailed fee & enrollment options.

How do I enrol my child?
+

Complete the enquiry form and our team will contact you to arrange a tour, explain fees, and complete admission steps. We support you through documentation and start dates.

What is the role of play in preschool learning?
+

Play is the primary way children learn: it builds language, problem solving, social skills and motor development. Our day includes guided & free play experiences.
