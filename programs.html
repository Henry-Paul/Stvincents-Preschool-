Curriculum & Programs

  • Sensorial play
  • Basic routines

Nursery

Target Age: 2.5 – 3.5 Years. Early literacy & numeracy activities.

  • Phonics awareness
  • Number recognition
  • Group engagement

Pre-Primary 1 & 2

Target Age: 3.5 – 6 Years. School readiness & independent learning.

  • Pre-writing & phonemic awareness
  • Basic arithmetic
  • Confidence & independence
